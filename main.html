Summary

In a future where Earth is ravaged by a global crop blight and massive dust storms, humanity is on the brink of extinction. A team of astronauts, led by former pilot Joseph Cooper, travels through a mysterious wormhole near Saturn to find a new habitable home for the human race in a distant galaxy.

  • Jessica Chastain as Murphy Cooper
  • Michael Caine as Professor Brand
